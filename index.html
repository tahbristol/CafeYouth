The Cultural Academy for Excellence (“CAFE”) is an award-winning music-based academic and life-skills enrichment program. We started in 1996 with the goal of encouraging young people to value learning and to perform well academically.

How do we do it?

After School Program

CAFÉ After School Academy (CASA) offers an exciting program for youth ages 6-14, to have the opportunity to realize their creative potential and to expand their artistic success into both academic and social achievement.

Music Education

CAFÉ Music Component is more than just an award winning Steel Orchestra. Students participate in a range of classes including Music Theory, Ear Training, Sight-Reading, and practical studies in technique, repertoire and improvisation.

Saturday Program

Our program provides engaging and differentiated instruction using multi-sensory techniques and materials that strengthen language arts and mathematics foundational skills; fostering confidence and achievement.
